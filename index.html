        ABOUT US

       

SHREE DESIGNER'S, is an eminent name among saree connoisseurs and has always made you glorious with flamboyant weaves in handloom sarees!
Having 16 years of rich heritage in Silk & Silk cotton sarees, all our sarees are handwoven and we are proud to say it!
Tradition for generations ‘Tradition for generations’ is what we believe and our collections reflect the same.

Our founder, Ms.CS LAVANYA takes immense effort in curating every saree and presents them without compromising quality, weaving at an affordable price. One of our specialties is we delight our customers with a wide variety in Silk cotton sarees. Hand block printed collections, Kuppadam collections - silk cotton with silk borders .

SHREE DESIGNERS online store is being launched with our signature Silk cotton sarees,Banaras Sarees, Casual wears & many more . we make sure every time you have a pleasant shopping experience with us online.
